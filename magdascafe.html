A Story in Every Cup

Welcome! I'm Magda. My journey with coffee began in the small, vibrant cafes of Europe and has led me here, to the heart of incredible India. I fell in love with the warmth of Bongaigaon and its people, and it became my dream to create a space where we could share stories over expertly brewed coffee.

Magda's is more than a café; it's a blend of cultures. We combine the rich traditions of Indian hospitality with the craft of global coffee culture. Our beans are sourced with care, our food is made with love, and our doors are always open to you.
